==============================================
       MY OPEN SOURCE MANIFESTO              
==============================================

  Author : codespace
  Date   : 20 March 2026

----------------------------------------------
  Every day, I rely on git -- a tool that someone
  built, shared, and gave to the world without asking
  for anything in return. That act of sharing is what
  open source means to me: it means choice.

  I believe that knowledge grows when it is shared,
  not hoarded. The best software in the world was
  built openly, collaboratively, and freely.

  One day, I want to build a study planner and release it
  as open source -- so that someone I will never meet
  can use it, improve it, and pass it forward.

  That is the open source way.

  -- codespace, 20 March 2026
==============================================
